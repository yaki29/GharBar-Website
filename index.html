Who are we?

Ghar bar is an Android based application that aims to ease the process of rental business. The housing sector still remains vigilant in facing the challenges of change by employing a new strategy that facilitates easy management of rental houses. Thus the application GharBar. It allows the user to connect with the Owner and receive new listings in the neighbourhoods that interest him/her with Apartment and Home Rental Search.

It would be an online solution for customers to find and rent houses from virtually anywhere. This apartment guide provides rental listing information to help you find your next rental home or apartment more easily than ever. This Application also enables the Owner to upload and share the genuine details of the place including photos and price, and also allows tenants to locate the location on a map.
